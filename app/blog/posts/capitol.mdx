  ## Dividend Discount Model (DDM) Valuation

  The Dividend Discount Model (DDM) was used to value Capitol Federal Financial Inc. (CFFN), focusing on the company’s consistent dividend payouts. The DDM provides a reliable valuation framework, particularly for companies like CFFN that have a strong history of returning value to shareholders through dividends.
 <ul className="my-6 ml-6 list-disc">
      <li>Current Market Price of CFFN is $10.95 per share, while the DDM model implies a value of $11.67 per share, suggesting the stock is undervalued by approximately 7%.</li>
      <li>Key inputs include a risk-free rate of 2.03%, market risk premium of 5.8%, and an adjusted beta of 0.53.</li>
      <li>Cost of Equity is calculated at 5.11%, and the WACC is determined to be 5.1%.</li>
      <li>Dividend per Share is $0.69, with a Dividend Payout Ratio of 171%, reflecting the company’s strategy of returning substantial earnings to shareholders.</li>
 </ul>
